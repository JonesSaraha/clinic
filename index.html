Meet Our Dental Team

Our experienced dental professionals are dedicated to providing you with the best care.

M.D.S in Conservative and Endodontic

DR. RAVIKUMAR M.D.S

Owner and Chief dentist

DR. RAVIKUMAR M.D.S is the owner and chief dentist in Aarthi Priyas Tooth Care Clinic. He has completed his M.D.S in Conservative and Endodontic at Ragas dental college in the year 2006. He has more than 20 years of experience in Root canal treatments and painless endodontic surgeries. He also has a strong academic career and has trained a number of postgraduate students in endodontics. He is currently a faculty of SRM DENTAL College.

M.D.S in Conservative and Endodontic

DR. MASSILLAMANI M.D.S

Co-owner

DR. MASSILLAMANI M.D.S is the Co-owner of Aarthi Priyas Tooth Care Clinic and has completed M.D.S in Oral Medicine & Radiology at Sri Ramachandra Dental College in 2009. She has both clinical and academic experience for more than 16 years. She has training in advanced imaging such as CBCT and C.T. Identification and treatment of cancer and precancerous lesions such as leukoplakia, lichen planus and oral submucous fibrosis. Management of chronic facial pain due to neuralgic, myofacial and temporomandibular joint disorders are her major priorities.
