Oral Cancer Screening

It is a quick and painless check-up done by the dentist to look for early signs of cancer in the mouth.

Early Detection Saves Lives

Comprehensive Examination

The dentist will look inside your mouth and feel your cheeks, tongue, and neck for any unusual changes or abnormalities.

Early Detection

Helps to detect cancer early, when it is easier to treat and has better chances of successful recovery.

Identifies Hidden Signs

Screening can find small sores, lumps, white/red patches that you may not notice during regular self-examination.

High-Risk Groups

It is especially important for people who smoke, chew tobacco, drink alcohol, or have long-standing mouth ulcers.

Quick Procedure

Takes just a few minutes and can be done during your regular dental check-up without any extra appointments.

No Preparation Needed

No special preparation needed before the screening - it's a simple visual and physical examination.

Fast Procedure

Takes only a few minutes during your regular dental visit

Painless

Completely non-invasive and comfortable examination

No Preparation

Ready to be screened during any dental check-up

Life-Saving Importance
Early detection through oral cancer screening can save lives and prevent major, invasive treatments. Regular screenings are crucial as they can identify pre-cancerous conditions before they develop into serious health problems, ensuring timely intervention and better treatment outcomes.
