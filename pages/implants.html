Dental Implants

The Modern Solution for Missing Teeth

Advanced Dental Implant Technology

A dental implant is an artificial tooth root placed into the jawbone to replace a missing tooth.

Biocompatible Material

It is made of biocompatible metal (usually titanium), so the body accepts it safely and integrates well with natural tissues.

Natural Integration

After placing the implant, the bone grows around it and makes it strong and stable, like a natural tooth root through osseointegration.

Complete Restoration

Once healed, a custom-made crown (artificial tooth) is fixed on top of the implant, restoring both function and aesthetics.

Healing Process

Healing time may range from 3–6 months, depending on bone quality, allowing for proper integration with the jawbone.

Recovery Timeline
3–6 Months
The healing period varies based on individual bone quality and density, ensuring proper osseointegration for long-term success.
