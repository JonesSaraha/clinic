Scaling and Polishing

Scaling and polishing is a professional dental cleaning procedure.
Professional Dental Cleaning

Removes Tartar and Plaque

Scaling cleans off hard deposits and soft plaque that regular brushing cannot remove, ensuring thorough cleaning below the gumline.

Prevents Gum Disease

By removing these deposits, scaling helps reduce bleeding gums, swelling, and bad breath, promoting healthier gums.

Polishing Makes Teeth Smooth

After cleaning, polishing removes minor stains and makes the tooth surface shiny and smooth, preventing future plaque buildup.

Quick and Painless Procedure

Usually takes 20–30 minutes and is comfortable for most patients, with no downtime required afterward.

Helps Maintain Oral Health

Doing scaling and polishing every 6 months keeps gums healthy and reduces the risk of future dental problems like cavities and gum disease.

Quick and Comfortable Treatment
The entire scaling and polishing procedure typically takes only 20-30 minutes, making it an efficient way to maintain optimal oral health with minimal time investment.

Recommended Every 6 Months

For optimal oral health, professional scaling and polishing should be done every 6 months. This regular maintenance prevents serious dental issues and maintains a healthy, bright smile.
