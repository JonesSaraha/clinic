Dental Aligners

The Clear, Modern Way to Straighten Your Smile

Nearly Invisible Teeth Straightening

Aligners are clear plastic trays used to straighten teeth, offering a discreet alternative to traditional braces.

Nearly Invisible

They are almost invisible, so most people don't notice them while you're wearing them.

Removable Design

You can remove them while eating and brushing, making oral hygiene much easier than with traditional braces.

Wear Time

You need to wear them for 20–22 hours a day for best results and effective treatment progression.

Progressive Treatment

New aligner trays are given every 10–14 days to slowly and gently move teeth into their desired positions.

No Wires or Brackets

Unlike traditional braces, aligners have no wires or brackets, providing greater comfort and aesthetics.

Treatment Duration

Treatment time varies depending on the case, but usually lasts from 6 months to 2 years.
