Traditional Metal Braces

Metal Braces

Most Common Type

Metal braces are the traditional braces used to straighten teeth effectively.

Very Strong and Durable

Made of stainless steel, they are less likely to break and work well for all kinds of tooth corrections.

Constant Gentle Pressure

They work by applying steady pressure to move teeth into proper alignment.

Rubber Band Colors Available

Patients can choose different colored elastic bands, making treatment more fun for kids and teens.

Affordable Orthodontic Option

Metal braces are usually the most economical choice compared to ceramic or invisible aligners.

Ceramic braces are made of tooth-colored or clear material, so they blend with teeth and look less visible.

Aesthetic Ceramic Braces

Ceramic Braces

Tooth-Colored & Aesthetic

Ceramic braces are made of tooth-colored or clear material, so they blend with teeth and look less visible.

Work Like Metal Braces

They give similar results to metal braces and are equally effective for most bite corrections.

Less Noticeable

Ideal for adults or patients who want a more aesthetic brace option.

More Delicate

Ceramic brackets can be more fragile compared to metal ones.

Slightly Costlier

They are usually more expensive than metal braces due to their cosmetic appearance.
