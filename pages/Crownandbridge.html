Crowns and Bridges

Dental Crowns and Bridges Restoration

Crown (Dental Cap)

A crown is a cap placed over a broken, weak, or root canal–treated tooth to protect it and make it strong.

Bridge

A bridge fills the gap created by one or more missing teeth by anchoring onto the nearby natural teeth.

They Look Like Natural Teeth

Crowns and bridges are made to match your tooth colour, shape, and size, giving a natural appearance that blends seamlessly with your smile.

Helps in Proper Chewing and Speaking

They restore the function of teeth, making chewing comfortable and improving speech clarity for better oral function.

Restorative Benefits
Crowns and bridges work together to restore both the appearance and function of damaged or missing teeth. While crowns protect weakened teeth, bridges replace missing ones - together they help maintain proper dental alignment, prevent shifting of remaining teeth, and restore confidence in your smile.
